The Regal Magic Gala

The RCEC offers a variety of premium magic programs for a variety of occasions. These shows are more splendorous, and are meant for medium to large audiences of around 50-100 in a close to staged enviroment. Think corporate events, weddings, celebrations, large private affairs where table to table or stage performances are wanted.

These premium shows are more customizable than our standard programs, like a semi-bespoke suit instead of fully bespoke.

Some company branding can be inserted into the show, as well as slogans, but for a much more focus and brand centered event we recommend our Royal Gala programs.

1. The Refined Magic Rendezvous Program is available for business trips starting at $700 CAD. (tax separate)
2. All shows will arive at the location half an hour to hour early to setup, audience will not be permitted in the area during this time. After the show all will be asked to withdraw to the 'waiting area' for 30 minutes as the space is cleaned and packed away.

5. Quotes will vary depending on distanced traveled outside of the magicians area, the time of booking (peak season), and geographical relation to Canada.
