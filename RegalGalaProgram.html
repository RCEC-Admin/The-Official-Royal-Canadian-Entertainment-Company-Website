The Regal Magic Gala

The RCEC offers a variety of premium magic programs for a variety of occasions. These shows are more splendorous, and are meant for medium to large audiences of around 50-100 in a close to staged enviroment. Think corporate events, weddings, celebrations, large private affairs where table to table or stage performances are wanted.
These premium shows are more customizable than our standard programs, like a semi-bespoke suit instead of fully bespoke.
Some company branding can be inserted into the show, as well as slogans, but for a much more focused and brand centered event we recommend one of our Royal Magic Jubilee programs.

Festival Program
This program offers a 40-minute show features the most visual and interactive magic shown in Canada! Hundreds gather at the drop of the hat in this program. This Magic connects and bringing it to your festival will make it the event of the year, ensuring your guests have the experience of a life-time!
