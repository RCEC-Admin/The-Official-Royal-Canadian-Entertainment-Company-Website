The Royal Magic Jubilee

The RCEC offers a variety of luxury magic programs for a variety of occasions. These shows are the most grandious and extravagent events, and are meant for medium to large audiences of around 100-500+ people in a stage enviroment. Think large corporate celebrations, extravagent private parties, or theatre shows.

NOTE

1. The Refined Magic Rendezvous Program is available for business trips starting at $1,800 CAD. (tax separate)
Estimates are based on 60 minute performances, and depending on budget, performances up to 100 minutes are held.
2. All shows will arive at the location an hour to two hours early to setup, audience will not be permitted in the area during this time. After the show all will be asked to withdraw to the 'waiting area' for 60 minutes as the space is cleaned and packed away.
3. The RCEC produces small-scale to large-scale shows, regardless, it is of immense help and witih lots of grattitude if you could cooperate/guide us to the closest possible parking available.
4. When performing in the middle of the event space, 2 minutes of preperation time for each tool or prop IN/OUT is required.
5. Quotes will vary depending on distanced traveled outside of the magicians area, the time of booking (peak season), and geographical relation to Canada.
