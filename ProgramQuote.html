A Variety of Shows!

The RCEC offers a large range of shows for Children, Youth, Teenagers, Families, Adults, small or large events, corporate or private parties, Brand events, opening cermonies, weddings, and pretty much anything else! We even have seen magic for funerals!

The booking process is outlined below.

Consultation/Inquiry

Depending on the purpose, schedule, audience, and budget of the event we can suggest the most suitable program. Consultation available by phone and email.

Booking/Confirmation

After reviewing the proposed program the reservation and confirmation of your tailored performance will be sent. We also will happily guide you through the details of the confirmation document.

Preparation and Planning

For high-quality stage productions we take the environment and the event location into careful consideration. This includes arriving early to prepare our performance equipment and coordinate directing elements.

Bespoke Assembly

The artist determines the purpose of your event and then customizes their content to be tailored to your mood. This ensures the we provide the highest level of immersion for you and your guests.

Performance and Payment

Upon arrival the performer(s) begin preperations, and once the audience is all seated begin the performance. After the event ends we encourage customer feedback and of course payment.
