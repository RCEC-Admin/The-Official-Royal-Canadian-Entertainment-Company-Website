The Royal Canadian Entertainment Company

The Royal Canadian Entertainment Company (hereinafter 'RCEC') is a Canadian magic entertainment brand.

The company has in its residence a variety of quality talented artists. We are constantly striving to be the best leading example in the world of magical entertainment content.

All our resident artists are striving to uphold the values and goals which create the
best entertainment possible.

In addition, RCEC adheres to all laws, and represents the ideals
foundational to Canada. We take the lead in fulfilling our responibilities
and obligations as members of the entertainment industry.

We do our best to inspire trust in our industry and company.
