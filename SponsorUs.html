What do we mean sponsor?

Well, we are a company, not as in a corporation, but rather a company can also mean a group of artists, and art rarely pays, infact the Romans knew this well. We get a couple words from our Roman friends: patronus meaning "defender, protector; former master (of a freed slave); advocate," and around 1300 it evolved into the word, patroun, "a lord-master, one who protects, supports, or encourages." So, in essence being a patron of an artist is to be their supporter and advocate.

So, if you wish to support The Royal Canadian Entertainment Company and you are a corporation, llc, or individual, etc... please get in contact with us at RoyalCanadianEntertainment@gmail.com with the subject line being: 'Sponsor'

As artists we will never 'sell-out' so if you want us to promote your brand, you should book one of our shows which offers that option, to sponsor in our case means donate funds to branch into new endevours, extend our talent pool, professional R&D, and/or maintain assests like this website!

Sincerely, from our hearts at the RCEC, we thank you for considering sponsoring us, not enough people smile these days and know that your support helps create those smiles people cherish.

Thanks, sincerely,
-Chris Wonder, The Prince of Entertainment, head of the RCEC
