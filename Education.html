The Imperial Magic Education Program

The RCEC offers a unique blend of our standard magic programs combined with educational content for a variety of a vareity of topics! Some of the topics include ACC (Alternative Career Choice) lectures regarding the performing arts, anti-smoking shows, English second-language shows (hopefully more languages in the future), and field degree lectures (our artists hold degrees in a variety of areas and often are happy to educate others on what they learned!) The company offers more than just these, so feel free to contact us if you don't see what you want and ask us!

ACC Lecture
This program offers a 25-minute show and a 15 minute lecture. It focuses on careers that are often not thought of as viable in the world, such as being a magician! Designed for junior high to post-secondary students it is engaging, fun, and ensures that your students get a professional's perspective.

Performance Consulation/Booking

Anti-Smoking/Substances Lecture
This program offers a 25-minute show and a 15 minute lecture. It focuses on the risks and data around smoking and drug use. Designed for junior high to post-secondary students it is engaging, captivating, and well-researched. We at the RCEC aim to inform your students with the best quality of research that we wanted as young adults!

Performance Consulation/Booking

ESL Program
This program offers a 20-minute basic-English show and a 20 minute English lecture. The show is designed for visual enjoyment, which doesn't depend on the audience having an advanced grasp of the English language. The show encorporates basic words, phrases and the lecture teaches any aspect of the English language you desire your students to learn!

Performance Consulation/Booking

Field-Degree Lecture
This program offers a 30-minute show and a 30 minute advanced lecture. The show is visual, interactive, and designed for the highschool, college, and university audience in mind. The lecture focuses on one of the degree fields in which our artists have graduated in. It offers post-grad perspectives and life experience which many students find insightful and beneficial.

Performance Consulation/Booking

Magic Camp
The RCEC offers a magic camp! This program could be a one time event OR a whole week! This camp focuses on the needs and desires of the students, from beginner to advanced.
The Imperial Royal Instructors aim to impart every bit of knowledge they can to the young-magicians they mentor.

Other Education Programs
The RCEC offers many other programs which we couldn't possibly list in this section. If you have a paticular subject you want a magic show and lecture for, by all means reach out to us and ask! The many entertainers at the RCEC retains are full of vast and varied life experience, and as studies have shown magic shows improve learning by 1000%!

NOTE

1. The Imperial Education Program is available for international and intranational trips starting at $400 CAD (tax separate) and up, depending on location of performer, time of booking, and location in the world.
2. All shows will arive at the location half an hour to hour early to setup, audience will not be permitted in the area during this time. After the show all will be asked to withdraw to the 'waiting area' for 30 minutes as the space is cleaned and packed away.
3. The RCEC produces small-scale to large-scale shows, regardless, it is of immense help and witih lots of grattitude if you could cooperate/guide us to the closest possible parking available.
4. When performing in the middle of the event space, 2 minutes of preperation time for each tool or prop IN/OUT is required.
5. Quotes will vary depending on distance traveled outside of the magician's area, the time of booking (peak season), and geographical relation to Canada.
