The Refined Magic Rendezvous

The RCEC offers a variety of standard magic programs for a variety of occasions. These shows are generally more budget friendly, but also are for smaller audiences of around 2-30 in a close to slightly distanced enviroment. Think restaraunt, speakeasies, birthdays, or parlour-style shows.
These standard shows are also not as customizable as our other programs, like an off-the-shelf suit as apposed to semi-bespoke and fully bespoke garments.

Refined Close-Up Card Conjuring
This program offers a 20-minute show featuring only the best card magic, mysterious cards traveling across impossible distances, the production of cards from thin air! This program truely has it all for those that are enthusiastic about true sleight-of-hand card magic!

NOTE

1. The Refined Magic Rendezvous Program is available for business trips starting at $400 CAD. (tax separate)
2. All shows will arive at the location half an hour to hour early to setup, audience will not be permitted in the area during this time. After the show all will be asked to withdraw to the 'waiting area' for 30 minutes as the space is cleaned and packed away.
3. The RCEC produces small-scale to large-scale shows, regardless, it is of immense help and witih lots of grattitude if you could cooperate/guide us to the closest possible parking available.
4. When performing in the middle of the event space, 2 minutes of preperation time for each tool or prop IN/OUT is required.
5. Quotes will vary depending on distance traveled outside of the magician's area, the time of booking (peak season), and geographical relation to Canada.
